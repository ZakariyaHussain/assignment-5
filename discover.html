Question-1: What are the different ways to select an element in the DOM?
There are five different way to select an element in the DOM.
i. document.getElementById("id name")
ii. document.getElementsByClassName("class name")
iii. document.getElementsByTagName("tag name")
iv. document.querySelector()
v. document.querySelectorAll()
Question-2: What is the difference between innerHTML, innerText, and textContent?
innerHTML means html tag in the selector while innerText is the text value in the selector and textContent is the other text out of the tag.
